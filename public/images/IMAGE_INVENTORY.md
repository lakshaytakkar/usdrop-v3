## Image Specifications

### Style Guidelines
- **Visual Style**: Mix of realistic professional photography and modern graphics
- **Color Palette**: Consistent warm/cool tones, professional lighting
- **Branding**: Subtle USDrop mentions (small text on clothing, equipment, or background elements)
- **Quality**: High-resolution, professional photography with realistic textures
- **Dimensions**: Various sizes optimized per use case (hero: 1920x1080, cards: 1200x800, thumbnails: 400x400, square: 1080x1080)

### Usage Notes
- All images include subtle USDrop branding
- Images maintain consistent lighting and color palette
- Realistic textures and natural imperfections for authentic feel
- Professional photography quality suitable for web use
