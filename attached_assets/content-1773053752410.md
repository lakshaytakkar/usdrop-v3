## Frequently Asked Questions

- ### Do I need a Shopify account?







No. A new account will be created with a ready-made store that you take over. This offer does not work with existing Shopify accounts, as many pre-existing settings can interfere with the conversion optimization.

- ### What if I already have a Shopify store?







If you already have a Shopify store, no problem! Having an expert build the store from scratch can make a big difference, as your current design might not be fully optimized for success. Tradelle’s team can create a new store for you that’s set up correctly from the start.

- ### Are there unmentioned fees?







No. However, once you take over the ready-made store account, a Shopify subscription will be required to keep it. It starts at $1/month for the first three months as a trial. There is no contractual obligation to prolong it, but then you lose access to your account/store.

- ### How many winning products will I get in my store?







100 hand-picked products. You can delete them, add your own, and use any dropshipping provider or supplier you like.


- ### What if I have my own products? Can I add more products to my store?







Yes! You can easily add as many of your own products to your store as you’d like after you receive it. The process is very straightforward and can be done with just a few clicks.

- ### Does Tradelle advertise my store?







No. Getting traffic to your site is your main task in dropshipping, be it through organic social media content, ads, or other marketing means.

- ### Are revisions or additional support included?







The $49 store comes fully optimized. We will not be able to further customize your store or offer tech support as this wouldn’t allow us to offer a price this low. However, you will receive a guide on how to claim and make changes to your store.

- ### Can I get a refund if I don’t like my store?







Yes. There is a 30-day no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. Just send a message to support@tradelle.io, and you will be refunded.
